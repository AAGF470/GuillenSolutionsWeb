Our story

Two hands, one small studio.

Ember & Field started at a kitchen table and a secondhand pottery wheel. Every candle is poured by hand in small batches; every piece of stoneware is thrown, trimmed, and glazed one at a time.

We make a little, we make it well, and we'd rather you keep something for ten years than replace it in one. That's the whole idea.
